Ascendron Technologies a company specialized in the business areas of Web Development, Mobile Application Development, Manpower Solutions, Training& Development, serving IT and non IT clients in Domestic and International sector, be it Service firms, Product companies, Technical support companies or start ups. We believe in the realization of our vision through our inherent values that have provided us with strength right from the outset. We represent a team of bright experienced professionals moving towards a common goal of achieving excellence and beyond. We provide innovative, time-bound solutions to our customers, among whom are some of the most prominent players in the industry. We aim to constantly add value to our customer’s requirements and expectations through our cutting edge technology solutions and our ability to understand their business processes. We believe that the most important factor of our success is the success of our clients,(and provide them with tools for successful business). Ascendron Technologies, both in idea and realization, is a client-oriented company. By supplying our customers with top resources and skills, we fulfill our main goal – add value to clients businesses. By remaining focused within specific industry sectors, we have developed an in-depth understanding of the nature of our clients’ business as well as their individual corporate culture. Our success depends on new ideas, so we constantly work towards ensuring that enterprising people succeed. Working with us means working with people who have a passion for achievement, strive for outstanding results and build effective relationships inside and outside our company.
